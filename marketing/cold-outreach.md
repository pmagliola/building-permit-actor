# Cold Outreach Templates — US Building Permit Scraper

## How to use these

1. Run the actor for the target company's city, filtered to their permit type
2. Pick 3-5 relevant permits from the results as examples
3. Personalise the template with those specific permits
4. Send via LinkedIn DM or email (find email via LinkedIn, Hunter.io, or their website)

Target companies: solar installers, roofing contractors, HVAC companies, construction lead gen firms.
Start with 20-30 outreach attempts, track response rate, iterate.

---

## Finding targets

**LinkedIn search:**
- "solar installer [city]" → filter to company pages → find owner/VP Sales
- "roofing contractor [city]" → same
- "HVAC contractor [city]" → same

**Google:**
- "[city] solar installation company" → get their contact page
- Use Hunter.io to find email if only website available

## Notes on personalisation

The single most important thing: include **real permit data from their city** in the first message. Generic outreach gets ignored. Specific data from their market demonstrates the tool works and gives them something concrete to react to.

Run the actor on their city first, pick 3 permits that match their profile (solar = new residential, roofing = re-roof or renovation, HVAC = new construction over $150k), paste them in.

Cost of one run to get sample data: under $0.10 for most cities.
